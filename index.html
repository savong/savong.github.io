Savong Bou (橋本 武彦)

Information

He is currently an Assistant Professor at the Center for Computational Sciences, University of Tsukuba, Japan. He received M.Eng., and Ph.D. degree in Engineering from the University of Tsukuba, Japan, in 2014, and 2017, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Hiroyuki Kitagawa and Prof. Toshiyuki Amagasa. His research interests include data cleansing, data integration, processing of data streaming, information retrieval, big data analysis, and machine larning/deep learning for real-time analytics.
